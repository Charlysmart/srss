Admission Process

Starting your journey with St. Raphael’s Secondary School begins with applying to sit for our entrance examination. Below is a detailed step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Start Your Application

To begin your application:

  • Apply online by clicking [HERE].
  • Alternatively, you may pick up a physical application form from any of the following locations:
    • St. Raphael’s Secondary School, Awkuzu.

Application fee details for both local and international applicants are provided on the online application form or at the collection point.

Step 2: Submit the Required Documents

The following documents must be submitted on or before the entrance examination date to complete your application:

  • A copy of the candidate’s most recent school result
  • Proof of payment for the application fee
  • A recent passport photograph of the candidate

Step 3: After the Examination

Candidates who perform successfully in the entrance exam will receive an admission offer letter, which will include:

  • A comprehensive fee guide and payment schedule for the academic session
  • A checklist of required items for newly admitted students

Step 4: Confirming Your Admission

To secure your child’s place:

  • Provide proof of payment of the registration fee
  • Pay any other fees outlined in the Fees Schedule
